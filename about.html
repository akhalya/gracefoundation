About Grace Foundation

Born from a simple belief — that every life, no matter how broken, deserves a chance at wholeness.

Our Story

A foundation built on the belief that hope is non-negotiable

Grace Foundation was established in Chennai with one guiding conviction: prevention is always better than cure — but no moment is too late for healing to begin.

We started with awareness campaigns in local schools, quickly realising the need ran deeper. Families were suffering in silence. Individuals were trapped in cycles of addiction with no visible way out. We had to do more.

Today Grace Foundation works across the full spectrum — inspiring young people to never start, and walking beside those fighting their way back. In partnership with Utopia Therapy and G Square, our reach continues to grow across Tamil Nadu.

Our Values

What guides everything we do

Compassion First

We meet people where they are — without judgment. Every person who reaches us deserves dignity, warmth, and the belief that change is possible for them.

Evidence-Based Care

Our programs are grounded in medical science, psychological research, and proven recovery models — not guesswork or stigma.

Community Power

Real change happens at community level. We build networks of parents, teachers, and local leaders who become the first line of support.

About the Foundation

Is Grace Foundation a registered NGO?
+

Yes. Grace Foundation is a registered charitable organisation operating in Tamil Nadu with full commitment to financial transparency.

Where are your rehabilitation centres?
+

Our primary operations are based in Chennai. We are actively expanding our rehabilitation network. Contact us for the nearest facility.

Do you work with government bodies?
+

Yes. We collaborate with government institutions, schools, and policy makers to scale awareness and advocate for evidence-based addiction policy.

How are donations used?
+

Donations fund rehabilitation for those who cannot afford care, school programs, family counseling, and youth scholarships. We maintain full transparency.
