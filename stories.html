Rajan's Return — A Father Reclaims His Life

"I told myself I'd stop after this last drink for eleven years. The day I walked into Grace Foundation, I finally understood — I couldn't do it alone, and I didn't have to."

Rajan (name changed) was 38 years old, a factory worker in North Chennai, when his wife brought him to our centre — not as a last resort, but as a last hope.

Eleven years of alcohol dependency had cost him his savings, his health, and nearly his family. Our team began with a full medical assessment, then a 30-day supervised detox, followed by six months of weekly group therapy and individual counseling.

Eight months after intake, Rajan walked his daughter to her first day of school — sober, present, and whole. Today he volunteers as a peer mentor, offering the same hand of hope that was once extended to him.

* Name and identifying details changed to protect privacy.
