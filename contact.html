Before You Reach Out

Common Questions

Is contacting Grace Foundation confidential?
+

Absolutely. All enquiries and personal information are treated with complete confidentiality. We will never share your details without your explicit consent.

Can I refer someone anonymously?
+

Yes. You can reach out on behalf of someone without disclosing your identity. We will guide you on how to approach the situation sensitively.

How quickly do you respond?
+

We respond to all enquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, please indicate this clearly and we will prioritise your response.

Do you cover all types of substance abuse?
+

Our programs address alcohol dependency and drug addiction, including prescription drug misuse. Each case is assessed individually for the appropriate level of care.
