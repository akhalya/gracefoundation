Campaign Overview

Taking the message where it's needed most

Short Films on Wheels was born from a simple insight: the communities most affected by alcohol abuse are often the least likely to attend formal awareness events. So we brought the events to them.

In partnership with G Square — India's largest plotted real estate developer, and a committed CSR partner — Grace Foundation deployed specially designed video vehicles across North Chennai from April to June 2025.

Each vehicle carried a screen, sound system, and a trained Grace Foundation counselor. After each screening of the Season 1 winning films, the counselor facilitated a 20-minute open Q&A session — giving community members a space to ask questions and share experiences they might otherwise have kept private.

The response was extraordinary. At several schools, students lined up after the screening to speak privately with the counselor. Two referrals to our rehabilitation program came directly from community members who attended outdoor public screenings.

"Two of the people who came to us for rehabilitation support this year heard about us for the first time from a vehicle parked outside their community hall. That is what this campaign means."
Grace Foundation Field Director
