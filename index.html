Why We Exist

Prevention is better than cure — but healing is always possible

Every day in Tamil Nadu, thousands of families are quietly torn apart by alcohol and substance abuse. Grace Foundation exists at that intersection — intervening before addiction takes hold, and walking alongside those already in its grip.

We believe in the dignity of every person and the resilience of every family. Our work is rooted in compassion, evidence, and the conviction that recovery is always within reach.

🛡

Prevent

School programs and community campaigns that reach young people before addiction does.

💊

Rehabilitate

Medical and psychological support for those walking the path back to wholeness.

🤝

Reintegrate

Family healing, vocational skills, and community support to make recovery last.

What We Do

Three pillars. One mission.

From first-day prevention to full reintegration — our programs cover the full arc of the journey.

01

Awareness & Prevention

We reach students, parents, and communities before the first encounter with substances — through school programs, public seminars, and creative film campaigns that build a culture of informed refusal.

Learn more →
02

Rehabilitation & Recovery

Medically supervised detox, psychological counseling, group therapy, and vocational training — all within a safe, non-judgmental environment designed for lasting recovery.

Learn more →
03

Youth & STEM Scholarships

We invest in young minds because giving youth purpose and aspiration is itself one of the most powerful forms of addiction prevention. Ambition is the antidote.
