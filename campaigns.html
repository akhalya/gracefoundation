Film Contest

Submission Guidelines

Everything you need to know to participate in Season 2.

1

Create Your Film

Short film up to 15 minutes on the negative effects of alcohol abuse. Tamil or silent format. Any genre — documentary, narrative, animation.

2

Upload to Drive or YouTube

Upload your film and generate a shareable link. Make sure the link is accessible to anyone with it.

3

Email Your Entry

Send the link with your full name, age, institution, and district to info@gracefoundation.co with subject: "Film Contest Season 2".

4

Winners Announced

Our panel reviews all submissions. Winners receive cash prizes, trophies, certificates, and their films are screened across Tamil Nadu.
