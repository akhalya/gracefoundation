Our Work

Preventing addiction before it starts. Healing those in its grip. Restoring families torn apart by it.

Pillar One

Awareness & Prevention

The most powerful intervention happens before the first drink. We reach young people, parents, and educators with clear, age-appropriate education about the realities of substance abuse.

Through school programs, public seminars, and mobile campaigns like "Short Films on Wheels" — in partnership with G Square — we are building a generation that chooses clarity.

🏫

📢

Community talks for parents, teachers, policymakers, and local leaders.

🚐

Video vehicles taking award-winning films directly to communities, schools, and public spaces.

Pillar Two

Rehabilitation & Recovery

For those caught in addiction, we provide medically sound, psychologically informed, and deeply human care. Recovery is not a single moment — it is a journey we walk together.

1

Assessment & Intake

Holistic assessment — physical health, mental health, family situation — to design a personalised recovery plan.

2

Medical Detoxification

Supervised medical detox under qualified healthcare professionals, ensuring safety throughout withdrawal.

3

Psychological Counseling

Individual and group therapy addressing the emotional roots of addiction and building coping skills for sobriety.

4

Family Healing

We work with families to rebuild trust, communication, and the support systems that make recovery sustainable.

5

Vocational Training

Practical skills and employment support to rebuild financial independence and a sense of purpose.

6

Reintegration Support

Long-term aftercare and relapse prevention planning to ensure recovery holds beyond our walls.

Pillar Three

Youth Empowerment & STEM Scholarships

Prevention isn't only about saying no — it's about giving young people something worth saying yes to. Our STEM scholarships and leadership programs create futures filled with ambition, community, and purpose.

When a young person has goals, mentors, and a community that believes in them, they are far less likely to turn to substances as an escape.

🔭

STEM Scholarships

Supporting young innovators who show promise and need support to reach their potential.

🌍

Leadership Programs

Developing ethical leaders from all socioeconomic backgrounds across Tamil Nadu.

🏆

Creative Competitions

Film and writing contests that channel youth energy into positive social impact.
